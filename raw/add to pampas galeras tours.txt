  <p class="snippet">The Pampas Galeras National Reserve was established in 1967 located in the province of Lucanas, department of Ayacucho, is 6,500 hectares, but its area of influence extends over 60,000 hectares and includes a sizable group of peasant communities. The goal is community development through sustainable resource management.</p>
  <p class="snippet">Its geography ranges from 3800 to 5000m.snm and average annual temperature is 5 º C.</p>
  <p class="snippet">The International Festival of the Vicuña, seeks to reassess the Andean culture, cultural practices that rescues the Pre-Inca techniques of management of the vicuña and is circling the extensive highland pastures with a human fence around the vicunas through music, song and spirited movements are driven toward the corral where they are classified as final and shearing to get your fibre, then releasing them without endangering their existence.</p> 
  <p class="snippet">This work is characterized by the impressive array of people involved: farmers, students from schools, colleges, universities, national and foreign professionals and general public. </p>
  <p class="snippet">Our guided tour starts at the time organized for your guide to pick you up from your hotel. the pampas galeras national reserve is located about 90 km from Nazca. </p>
