The Coast of Peru

Crown of South America's Ruta del Sol, Peru's section of Pacific coastline is unrivaled on the continent.

Discover Peru's unique coast in all it's majesty, abundance, and history. Lima - a city of kings awash in gold and ceremony. To the south: Paracas - Peru's Galapagos, Huacachina - a desert oasis, mystery of the Nasca lines; Huaraz and ancient Chan Chan to the north.

Tours in Lima

Lima City Tour

The Axis of Latin-American history, Lima was also the epicenter of Incan royal ceremony and rule.

Pachacamac Tour

A guided tour of the vast archaeological citadel of Pachacamac, the temple of the deity "Pachacamac" - believed to be the creator of the universe.

Caral Tour

An archaeological adventure just two hours from Lima to discover the Norte Central civiliation - the oldest in the Americas.

Peruvian Gold

See Peru’s most famous gold collection, a vast and dazzling array of ancient Peruvian gold and the Arms of the World Collection.

Museums of Lima

A tour that comprises breathtaking colonial mansions housing probably the best gold, ceramic, textile, and art collections in South America.

Lima Nocturnal

The only tour that includes a show and buffet dinner - discover the beauty of Peru's capital, visiting main attractions and architectonic monuments.
Paracas & Islas Ballestas

Paracas Reserve

Astounding biological diversity generated largely by antarctic currents - Peru's Galapagos.

Islas Ballestas

Paracas's premier wildlife attraction. Many exotic species of birds and marine life, some unique to Peru.

Paracas Dune Buggy Tour

Action and adventure - a roller coaster thrill ride of mountainous sand dunes by buggy

Huacachina Sandboarding

Nasca Tours

Nasca Lines

An archaeological adventure just two hours from Lima to discover the Norte Central civiliation - the oldest in the Americas.

Pampas Galeras Reserve

The Pampas Galeras Reserve is the focal point for conservation of Vicuña in Peru. Their fibre is considered the finest in the world.

Cantolloc Aqueducts

The aqueducts of Cantalloc are Nazca's greatest achievement

Cemetary Chaucilla

Prehispanic mummies and ancient artifacts of great archaeological significance are found just outside of Nazca city
North Coast Tours

Snorkel with Turtles

A small fishing village in the north of Peru, El Nuro where EcOceánica hosts a conservation program for sea-turtles

Mangroves in Tumbes

The Tumbes mangroves (Manglares de Tumbes) are a refuge for the American Crocodile, vulnerable to extinction, and many other species of fish, mammals and birds

Tour Chan Chan

Constructed by the Chimor (the kingdom of the Chimú), a civilization which grew out of the remnants of the Moche civilization, Chan Chan is a mysterious archaelogical wonder and UNESCO World Heritage site

Huaca de la Luna

Huaca de la Luna (Temple of the Moon) was built by the ancient Moche culture and was their ceremonial center
