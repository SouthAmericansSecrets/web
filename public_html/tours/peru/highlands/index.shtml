The Highlands of Peru

Breathtaking mountain scenery and lively traditions, the Andean culture of Peru is steeped in ancient mystery.

Visit the bizarre floating city Uros in the world's highest navigable lake Titicaca (Puno), trek trails traversed by ancient Incas to the Sacred Valley and Machu Picchu, observe a most beautifully preserved colonial city in Arequipa and take in the stunning Colca Canyon nearby.

Arequipa Tours

Arequipa City Tour

A Spanish colonial marvel and UNESCO World Heritage Site, Arequipa is also known as "The White City" for it's vast array of colonial architecture built from sillar - a pearlish-white volcanic rock.

Colca Canyon Tour

One of the world's deepest canyons, Colca is astoundingly beautiful and cannot be missed - not just for it's scenic beauty but also it's vibrant indigenous culture.
