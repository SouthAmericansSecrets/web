Activities in Paracas and surrounding

So much to do, so little time...

This very familiar phrase is never more applicable than when in Paracas. There is literally something for everyone - no matter what the weather or season.

Sightseeing, biking, sailing, kitesurfing, sandboarding, birding or just hanging out - Paracas has it all for thrill-seekers young and old. Don't miss Paracas and plan to stay awhile!

Showcased Tours

Arequipa

A Spanish colonial marvel and UNESCO World Heritage Site, Arequipa is also known as "The White City" for it's vast array of colonial architecture built from sillar - a pearlish-white volcanic rock.

Puno Tour

Between mountains and the shores of Lake Titicaca lies the folkloric capital of Peru, named so for its rich culture, art and dance. Nearby, encounter the strange floating town of the Uros...

Cuzco Tour

The famous jumping point for trekkers to Machu Piccu, Cuzco is the historical capital of the Inca Empire and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Islas Ballestas

Spectacular bird and marine life overflowing on speedboat ride to these very unique islands.

Paracas Reserve

Amazing geological formations and internationally famous bird watching. Also ancient lost civilization Paracas culture nearly forgotten.
